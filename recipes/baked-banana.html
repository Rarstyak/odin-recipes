Baked Banana Wrap

Description

These are the basic sweet banana wraps. They are bananas wrapped in sweet rice baked within banana leaves. The bananas are sweet and soft, while the rice sticks too your teeth due to the crunchy outer texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sweet rice soaked overnight
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 5 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconuts
  • bananas
  • banana leaves

Steps

  1. Mix sweet rice, salt, coconut milk, sugar, and shredded coconuts.
  2. Coat banana leaves with mixture and place bananas on top.
  3. Wrap the bananas to have an even coating of the mixture and fully envoloped, tightly by the banana leaves.
  4. Bake for 350 degrees for 20 min.
  5. Cover with aluminum foil.
  6. Broil for 20-25 min until golden brown.
