Apple Cake

Description

Some apple bread.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • brown sugar
  • cinnamon
  • 4 apples

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients except brown sugar and cinnamon, which are sprinkled on top.
  2. Bake for 350 degrees for 40 min or Air Fry at 320 degrees for 30 min.
