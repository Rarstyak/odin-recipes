Pumpkin Desert

Description

Soft pumpkin bars.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups shredded pumpkin
  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cup coconut milk cream
  • banana leaves

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Wrap tightly into banana leaves.
  3. Steam for 40 min.
